How to Play

1. Start Your Journey

Begin by entering your name and reading the story introduction to understand your mission.

2. Explore the World

Navigate through different locations using the map interface. Each location holds unique challenges.

3. Solve Puzzles

Interact with objects and characters to solve challenges that unlock new areas and story elements.

4. Discover Secrets

Find hidden artifacts and uncover the mystery of the Sky Compass to complete your adventure.
